Baron Nashor





Baron Nashor is the most powerful neutral Monster icon monster on Summoner's Rift icon Summoner's Rift. It is located in the baron pit, which inherits the monster's namesake. Elemental Dragon





The Elemental Dragons are a crucial part of a League of Legends game, empowering the team that slays them with game-changing buffs. The game has six elemental dragons, each embodying a distinct essence: Chemtech, Cloud, Hextech, Infernal, Ocean, and Mountain. The stakes soar to new heights as two teams clash in the dragon pit to secure this all-important objective.

Rift Herald





Rift Herald is a powerful neutral Monster icon monster in Summoner's Rift icon Summoner's Rift. It is located in the baron pit. Slaying the Rift Herald drops the Eye of the Herald Eye of the Herald at the entrance of the pit for 20 seconds, which can be picked up by one member of the slayer's team, and is used for summoning the Rift Herald Mercenary. Upon picking it up, the slayer's team also gains Glimpse of the Void Glimpse of the Void.
